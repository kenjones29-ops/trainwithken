Superset · Antagonist Pairing

Pair A1 and A2 back-to-back with no rest between exercises. Rest 90 seconds between full rounds.

Superset A — 4 Rounds
  • A1: Barbell Bench Press8–10
  • A2: Bent-Over Barbell Row8–10
Superset B — 3 Rounds
  • B1: Seated DB Shoulder Press10
  • B2: Lat Pulldown12
Form Cue: On every press, retract scapula first. On every row, pull elbow back — don't flex with your bicep. Quality over weight every rep.
90 sec between rounds

Post-Session Protocol
FUEL &
RECOVER
Within 60 Minutes Post-Workout
🥩 Protein: 35–50g fast-digesting protein. Whey shake + 8oz milk, or chicken breast + rice. Start MPS now.
🍚 Carbs: 50–80g simple/fast carbs. White rice, banana, honey, or a sports drink. Replenish glycogen.
💧 Water: Minimum 20oz immediately. Add electrolytes if you sweat heavily.
Tonight Before Bed
🌙 Casein Protein: 30–40g slow-digesting protein — cottage cheese, Greek yogurt, or casein shake. Muscle grows while you sleep.
😴 Sleep: 7–9 hours. Growth hormone peaks in deep sleep. That's when today's work pays off.
