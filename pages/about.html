Background

FROM THE
FIELD TO
THE STAGE

Football came first. College, then pro. That's where I learned that talent is cool but discipline is what actually puts you on the field — same thing that puts you on stage.

After football I went all in on bodybuilding and earned my IFBB Pro card in Men's Physique. Right now I'm prepping for the 2026 Olympia Open in Las Vegas and the 2027 Masters Olympia in Japan — same protocols I run my clients through.

Master's in Healthcare Admin from Texas Southern. So when I tell you something about your body, it's not just gym bro talk — there's a reason behind it.

Philosophy

PRO-BUILT
SCIENCE-
BACKED

I'm not teaching you stuff I read in a book. I'm coaching you off the same playbook I use to step on the Olympia stage — just adjusted to your life, your body, and what you actually want.

Nutrition is ounce-based. Sounds intense, but it just means you stop guessing. We pair that with InBody scans so we're tracking actual body comp, not just the bathroom scale.

Goal isn't just to make you look better. Goal is for you to walk away knowing your body well enough that you never need a coach again.
