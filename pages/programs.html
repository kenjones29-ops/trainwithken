The Method

THE KJ FRAMEWORK

01
InBody Analysis

We start with an InBody scan so we know exactly where you're at — muscle, body fat, hydration, metabolic rate. No guessing. From there, we measure what changes.

02
Ounce-Based Macros

My nutrition system is ounce-based. You weigh your food, hit your targets, done. Sounds tedious — takes a week to become second nature, and it works.

03
Recovery Protocol

You don't grow in the gym, you grow when you recover. Sleep, active recovery, and a supplement protocol that actually fits what your body needs — not what the influencer is selling.

04
Accountability System

Every week you check in. Numbers, photos, how training felt. I read it, I respond. That's the part most programs skip — and that's the part that makes you actually finish.

Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need to train in Houston to work with KJ?
Nope. I coach people all over the world. Online Coaching and Competition Prep are 100% remote. The only one that requires you being in Houston is the Small Group Training at Level One.
How long until I see results?
Real talk: if you do the work, you'll feel a difference in 3–4 weeks and see it by week 6. Most of my clients hit their first big milestone around 12–16 weeks. If you're inconsistent, all bets are off.
Is the nutrition plan flexible for my diet preference?
Yes. I give you the targets — you pick the foods. Meat, plant-based, allergies, religious restrictions — doesn't matter. We work with what you eat.
What equipment do I need?
Default is a commercial gym setup — bars, dumbbells, cables, machines. If you train at home or have a weird setup, tell me on our first call and I'll write your program around what you have.
Can I switch plans after I start?
Of course. A lot of my people start with Online Coaching and graduate into Competition Prep when they're ready to step on a stage. Switch happens at your next billing date.
Is there a free trial or discovery call?
Yeah. I do a free 20-minute call for anyone serious about Online Coaching, Competition Prep, or the Coach's Curriculum. Book a slot on the booking page. We just talk — no hard sell.
